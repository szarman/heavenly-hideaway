🌮 Dos Amigos (Boone, NC – ~25 minutes away)

Looking to feed a big group without breaking the bank? Check out Dos Amigos, located just down Highway 194 as you enter Boone. They offer generous portions at great prices, making it a solid option for families or hungry hikers.

Owner’s Tip: Don’t miss their margaritas — they’re excellent and surprisingly affordable. A reliable go-to when you're craving Mexican food.

📍 Directions to Dos Amigos

🥪 Macado's (Boone, NC – ~25 minutes away)

If you're visiting Boone for the day, check out Macado's for lunch or dinner. They offer a vast menu with fantastic sandwiches, making it a great spot to refuel during your adventures.

📍 Directions to Macado's

🍺 Boondocks Brewing (West Jefferson, NC – ~20 minutes away)

Boondocks Brewing is a local favorite with two downtown locations: the Tap Room & Restaurant and the Brew Haus. They offer a wide selection of craft beers and a diverse menu, often with live music and events.

Owner’s Tip: Try their blackened shrimp tacos—they're some of the best I've had.

📍 Directions to Boondocks Brewing

🍻 New River Brewing (West Jefferson, NC – ~20 minutes away)

New River Brewing offers a rustic setting with amazing wings, burgers, and brews. They serve up fresh, local flavors that highlight the mountain vibe of the area.

Owner’s Tip: Their pulled pork sandwich is tender and flavorful — a must-try.

📍 Directions to New River Brewing

🍔 Black Jack’s Pub & Grill (West Jefferson, NC – ~20 minutes away)

Black Jack’s is a cozy pub known for creative burgers and friendly vibes. A casual place to grab a beer and a great meal downtown.

Owner’s Tip: Build your own burger and don’t miss their wings!

📍 Directions to Black Jack’s

🍖 The Pedalin' Pig (Boone, NC – ~25 minutes away)

The Pedalin' Pig brings real North Carolina BBQ to Boone with slow-smoked meats and bold flavors. Brisket, ribs, pulled pork — it's all good.

Owner’s Tip: The pulled pork is tender and delicious, and their variety of sauces means every bite is a little different.

📍 Directions to The Pedalin' Pig
