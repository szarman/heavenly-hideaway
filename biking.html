🚲 Todd Railroad Grade Road + Big Hill Rd

Looking for a relaxed, low-effort ride? Rent a bike from RiverGirl Fishing Co. and hop on Todd Railroad Grade Road — a gently sloping, paved route that runs alongside the New River. Great for families or casual riders, this road takes you through peaceful countryside and offers plenty of picnic and photo spots.

If you're up for a bit more exploration, cross the river and ride along Big Hill Rd. Despite the name, the first stretch is flat and scenic, running parallel to the river and shaded by trees. It's another peaceful, beautiful ride.

🚴 Virginia Creeper Trail (Damascus, VA – ~1.5 hours away)

Don’t let the 24-mile distance intimidate you — the Virginia Creeper Trail is one of the easiest and most scenic rides you’ll ever do. Rent bikes in Damascus, take the shuttle to the top, and then coast 24 miles downhill through forests, farmland, and bridges all the way back into town. You’ll barely have to pedal.

Owner’s Tip: Bring water, snacks, and a camera. It’s a relaxing half-day trip that delivers serious scenery and fun. One of the most memorable activities in the region.
