🎶 Todd Summer Concert Series (Todd, NC – ~5 minutes away)

The Todd Summer Music Series is a free, family-friendly outdoor concert series held at Cook Memorial Park in downtown Todd. Concerts take place on select Saturdays throughout the summer, featuring a variety of musical genres. Attendees are encouraged to bring a chair or blanket to sit on. Well-behaved, leashed dogs are welcome. Food, including hamburgers, hotdogs, veggie burgers, chips, and cookies, is available for purchase.

2025 Concert Lineup:

  • June 28, 6–8 PM – The Lucky Strikes
  • July 4, 11:30 AM–1:30 PM – The King Bees (following the Todd Liberty Parade)
  • July 19, 6–8 PM – Wayne Henderson & Friends
  • August 2, 6–8 PM – The Burnett Sisters
  • August 16, 6–8 PM – Sassafras
  • August 30, 6–8 PM – Jeff Little Trio

Owner’s Tip: Arrive early to secure a good spot and enjoy the community atmosphere. Don’t forget to bring cash for the food vendors.

🎉 More Boone Events & Festivals

Looking for even more ways to celebrate in the High Country? Explore a full lineup of annual events in Boone, including:

  • 🎨 Art in the Park – A juried art show series held monthly from May through October.
  • 🎺 High Country Jazz Festival – A weekend of jazz performances across Boone venues.
  • 🎭 Horn in the West – The nation's longest-running Revolutionary War outdoor drama.
  • 🚴 Blood, Sweat, and Gears – A challenging cycling event through the Blue Ridge Mountains.
  • 🎻 Doc Watson Day Celebration – Honoring the legendary folk musician with live performances.
  • 🎇 Boone 4th of July Parade – A festive parade through downtown Boone.
  • 🎶 Symphony by the Lake – An outdoor concert set against the backdrop of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

🎶 West Jefferson Events & Festivals

West Jefferson, nestled in Ashe County, offers a delightful array of events throughout the year, celebrating music, food, and community spirit. Here are some highlights:

  • Backstreet Concert Series: Enjoy free live music every Friday evening during the summer at Backstreet Park. This series features bluegrass, blues, and classic rock by local and regional artists.
  • Ashe Bash Concert: An annual music festival bringing together a diverse lineup of musicians for a day of entertainment, food, and fun in the heart of West Jefferson.
  • Ashe County Farmers Market: Held weekly, the market includes fresh produce, artisan crafts, food vendors, and often live music — a lively spot for both locals and visitors.
  • Christmas in July Festival: A unique summer event that combines holiday spirit with summer fun — complete with music, food trucks, arts and crafts, and a parade downtown.

For a full calendar of events, visit the Ashe County Chamber of Commerce Events Page.
