Welcome!

We hope you enjoy your stay at Heavenly Hideaway. To ensure a peaceful and enjoyable experience for everyone, please review and follow these simple house rules:

General Rules

  • No smoking inside the cabin. (Smoking outdoors is allowed in designated areas only. Please dispose of cigarette butts properly.)
  • No pets allowed without prior approval.
  • No parties or events.
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM. Please respect our neighbors and the peaceful mountain setting.
  • Only registered guests are allowed on the property overnight.

Property Care

  • Please treat the cabin and grounds with care.
  • Report any damages or maintenance issues promptly.
  • Do not rearrange furniture.
  • Do not leave food outdoors (to avoid attracting wildlife - while rare, bears have been spotted in the area and would love your leftovers).

Check-In and Check-Out

  • Check-in: After 4:00 PM
  • Check-out: By 10:00 AM
  • Self check-in with keypad lock.
  • Before you leave, please:
    • Place all used towels in the laundry area or bathtub.
    • Remove sheets from all beds used, place them in the laundry area, and start a load of laundry.
    • Load and start the dishwasher if any dishes were used.
    • Bag all trash and place it into the large trash cans inside the garage.
    • Lock all doors and windows when you leave.

Hot Tub Rules

  • Shower before entering the hot tub.
  • No glass in or around the hot tub.
  • Children must be supervised at all times.
  • Please replace the hot tub cover when not in use.
  • No food, oils, or bubbles in the hot tub.
  • Use the hot tub at your own risk.

Fire Pit Rules

  • Never leave the fire unattended.
  • Fully extinguish the fire before leaving or going inside.
  • Follow local fire restrictions if applicable.

Fireplace Use

  • Please ensure the flue is open before starting a fire.
  • Use only provided or approved firewood (no trash or plastics).
  • Never leave the fireplace unattended while burning.
  • Do not use accelerants such as lighter fluid.
  • Make sure the fire is completely out before going to bed or leaving the cabin.

Connectivity

Wi-Fi is available. Login details are provided in the House Manual inside the cabin.

Liability Notice

Guests are responsible for their own safety during their stay. Use of the property, hot tub, fire pit, fireplace, and creek areas is at your own risk. The host is not responsible for accidents, injuries, or loss of personal belongings.

Thank you for respecting our home! We hope you enjoy your time at Heavenly Hideaway.
