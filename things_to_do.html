🚣‍♂️ Water Adventures on the New River

There’s nothing more refreshing than a day on the New River, especially in the warmer months — and Heavenly Hideaway is just minutes from two of the best outfitters in the High Country.

🎣 RiverGirl Fishing Co. (Todd, NC – 5 minutes away)

RiverGirl Fishing Co. is a beloved local outfitter offering kayak, tube, and bike rentals in the quaint town of Todd. On hot summer days, floating down the New River in a tube is pure bliss. The route takes you through a peaceful, shaded stretch of the river — perfect for couples, families, and anyone looking to unwind.

Tip: Visiting in the spring or fall? Opt for a kayak — the water’s cold, but the views are beautiful.

📍 Directions to RiverGirl

🛶 Zaloo’s Canoes, Kayaks & Tubes (Jefferson, NC – ~20 minutes away)

Zaloo’s provides access to a deeper, wider section of the river. Ideal when water levels are low or you want a new route, this scenic float takes you through farmland and mountain views with calm currents.

📍 Directions to Zaloo’s

💡 Owner’s Helpful Hint: DIY Tubing

You can purchase cheap inner tubes and a 12V air pump at Walmart. Launch your own float from Green Valley Community Park and float ~1.5–2 hours back to Todd. Use two vehicles: one parked in Todd, and one to shuttle your group to the park. It’s a guest favorite!

⛳ Mountain Golfing Near Heavenly Hideaway

🏔️ Mountain Aire Golf Club (West Jefferson, NC – ~20 minutes away)

Mountain Aire is a scenic mountain course with elevation drops, fair but challenging play, and long-range views. It’s the closest course to the cabin and a favorite among locals and guests alike.

Owner’s Tip: Rent an extra cart for non-golfers to ride along and enjoy the mountain beauty — it’s like a scenic hike without the walking.

📍 Directions to Mountain Aire

🏌️‍♂️ Olde Beau Golf & Country Club (Roaring Gap, NC – ~1 hr 15 min away)

Olde Beau is a pristine, well-manicured mountain golf course that feels like a private club — yet it's affordable and unforgettable. Worth every minute of the 1 hr 15 min drive.

Owner’s Tip: Take the Blue Ridge Parkway back via Deep Gap for a scenic end to your golf day.

📍 Directions to Olde Beau

🚴‍♀️ Scenic Biking Adventures

🚲 Todd Railroad Grade Road + Big Hill Rd

Rent a bike from RiverGirl Fishing Co. and enjoy a peaceful ride down Todd Railroad Grade Road, a mostly flat route that hugs the New River. Cross the bridge for an equally scenic and flat ride along Big Hill Rd — don’t let the name fool you!

📍 Directions to RiverGirl

🚴 Virginia Creeper Trail (Damascus, VA – ~1.5 hours away)

This 24-mile downhill ride is a must-do. Rent a bike from The Bike Station in Damascus and take their shuttle to the top. From there, it’s a gentle coast through forests, fields, and bridges back into town. You’ll barely need to pedal.

Owner’s Tip: Bring a camera, snacks, and enjoy the most relaxing 24-mile ride you’ll ever experience.

📍 Directions to The Bike Station
